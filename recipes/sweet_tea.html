Sweet Tea

Description


Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of water
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 7 tea bags

Directions

  1. Pour water into a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Remove water from heat and add tea bags to pot
  3. Let tea brew till desired strength is reached
  4. Add 2 cups of sugar to tea
  5. Stir a few times
  6. Enjoy
