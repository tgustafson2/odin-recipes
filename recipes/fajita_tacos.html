Fajita Tacos

Description


This is recipe for some delicious tacos

Ingredients

  • Marinated skirt steak or chicken
  • Yellow Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tortillas
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Chop up the onions, bell peppers, and mushrooms
  2. Cover with avocado oil, and place in aluminum foil
  3. Place on grill at 400 degrees for 10 minutes flipping every 2 minutes
  4. Grate cheese
  5. Chop up lettuce
  6. Grill meat for two minutes a side for steak, for chicken cook until done
  7. Warm tortillas on comal
  8. Add desired toppings
  9. Enjoy
